Hi! I'm a Postdoc in the group Structure of evolution, at the Max Planck Institute for Mathematics in Sciences (Leipzig, Germany). I currently work on computational method to design RNA molecules. Those methods rely on statistical physics, neural-network, or dynamic programming.

After a Master in Bioinformatics at Paris-Saclay university (2015-2017), I started a PhD in biology at Ecole Polytechnique (Institut Polytechnique de Paris). From 2017 to 2020, I worked on computational protein design under the supervision of Thomas Simonson in the Structural Biology of the Cell. I defended my PhD the 29th of October 2020.
